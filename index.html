Intro to CSS

Read through the list of prompts below and try to complete each one by manipulating the styles.css file. To help you along your way, it is highly recommended that you open up your the developer tools of your browser to test out different properties and values as you work, and don't be afraid to consult a search engine or your classmates sitting near you for help.

Your prompts:

  • change the background color of the webpage
  • define a new color for all of the text
  • change the size of the top level heading
  • alter the weight of the sub-heading
  • limit the width of the paragraph
  • add some space between the edges of the browser and the content
  • put a border around the content
  • give your paragraph some indentation
  • increase the amount of space after your paragraph
  • change the look of this list
  • make the sub-heading all uppercase
  • change the tracking (letter spacing) of a heading
  • alter the appearance of the hyperlink
